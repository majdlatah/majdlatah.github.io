Majd Latah


Currently, I work as a postdoctoral researcher at the department of Informatics of the University of Hamburg.
I have recieved my PhD from the department of Computer Science at Ozyegin University under the supervision of Dr. Kübra Kalkan.
My research interest is network security with a focus on software-defined networking and blockchains.
