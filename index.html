Majd Latah

After completing my PhD, I worked as a research associate at the Department of Informatics of the University of Hamburg, where I contributed to the RESISTANT project.
I have received my PhD from the Department of Computer Science at Ozyegin University under the supervision of Dr. Kubra Kalkan.
My research interest is network security with a focus on software-defined networking and blockchains.
You can contact me at: latahmajd@gmail.com
