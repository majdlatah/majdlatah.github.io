Majd Latah

After completing my PhD, I worked as a research associate at the Department of Informatics of the University of Hamburg, where I contributed to the RESISTANT project. I received my PhD from the Department of Computer Science at Ozyegin University under the supervision of Dr. Kubra Kalkan.
